My biggest lesson that I have reflected on in my afterlife is the constant battle between nature and nurture. In order for Copper and I to maintain our bond, we had to fight our natural roles as hunter and prey. When our friendship was most tested was when our instincts were acting the strongest. Our friendship was tragic yet inescapable. Although our friendship gave nature a run for her money, it was in our natural predator prey roles in which led my time to ultimately expire. I wouldn't have wanted to go out being chased by any other hound dog.
